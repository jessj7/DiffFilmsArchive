    • DiffFilms Archive

    Running since 2009, DiffFilms has a long history of filmmaking within Cardiff University. It has provided opportunities for students at Cardiff University for 10 years, allowing them to try filmmaking and discover a new hobby, career and make friends along the way.

    This archive website aims to document what has been created by the society in order that potential new members or clients can see the variety of work that has been accomplished over the years.
